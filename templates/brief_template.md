## How to read this brief (legend)

Every analyzed post and every idea carries a **badge** and a **metric chip** so you know *why*
it's here and *what to do with it*.

**Badges**
- 🔥 **TREND** — a breakout post (beat its author's own normal by a lot). *Ride it this week while it's hot.* Time-sensitive.
- 🏗 **DURABLE** — an author who reliably wins. *Their repeatable formula — build it into the rotation.* Evergreen.
- ⭐ **PEAK** — both at once (a proven author hitting a new high). *Strongest signal we have.*

**Metric chip** — `breakout 2.4× · baseline p38 · ⚠ verify`
- `breakout N×` — how far the post beat its *own author's* median engagement (the spike).
- `baseline pNN` — where that author's sustained engagement ranks vs everyone we track (the consistency).
- `⚠ verify` — flag: engagement looks reply-driven (possibly controversy/ratio). Sanity-check before modeling.

**Confidence**
- `high` — pattern proven across many posts / durable authors. Safe to build on.
- `provisional` — seen once, in a single breakout. Treat as a hypothesis; ride but don't bet the rotation on it.

### 🔥 Ride Now
- **Post:** [`@ridd_design` — "Your onboarding isn't too long. It's too vague."](https://x.com/ridd_design/status/000)
